Welcome to Queen Rice

About us
Queen Rice Vina
Co. Ltd

Queen Rice is a leading Manufacturing and directly supplying with a wide range of high-quality rice products sourced from Vietnam. We offer all types of rice originating from the fertile lands of this Southeast Asian country. Our commitment to customer satisfaction has established us as a trusted name in the global rice market.

"We understand the diverse culinary preferences of customers worldwide. That's why we provide a comprehensive selection of rice varieties, including Fragrant Jasmine rice, Sushi rice, Japonica rice, Calrose rice, long-grain white rice, ST25 rice, sticky glutinous rice, and nutritious Brown rice, Red rice, Black rice, among others. Our aim is to deliver rice products that meet the highest standards of taste, aroma, and nutritional value."

Ms Don - Company President

"We prioritize quality throughout our operations. From the careful sourcing of rice to the meticulous processing and packaging, we ensure that our customers receive rice of the highest quality. Our close partnerships with local farmers and cooperatives in Vietnam's fertile regions enable us to maintain sustainable farming practices and responsible water management, resulting in exceptional rice crops."

Ms Don - Company President

"Sustainability is at the core of our business. We promote environmentally friendly practices, including the use of natural fertilizers, minimizing pesticide use, and implementing efficient irrigation systems. By prioritizing sustainability, we contribute to the preservation of Vietnam's natural resources and protect the ecological balance of the regions where rice is cultivated."

Ms Don - Company President

"As a rice manufacturer, we take pride in our reliable supply chain and timely delivery to customers worldwide. Our dedicated sales team is committed to providing personalized service and ensuring that customers' unique requirements are met. We believe in transparent and ethical business practices, fostering trust and long-term relationships with our customers, suppliers, and stakeholders"

Ms Don - Company President
